📖 Read the chapter

Factors divide in exactly; multiples appear in the times-table; a prime has exactly two factors. These ideas power fraction simplifying, HCF and LCM.

A factor divides a whole number exactly, with no remainder; a multiple is a number you land on when counting up in that number's times table.

  • A prime number has exactly two factors: 1 and itself. 1 is not prime, because it only has one factor. For example, 7 has only 1 and 7 as factors, so it is prime.
  • A composite number has more than two factors — it is neither prime nor equal to 1. Every whole number greater than 1 is either prime or composite. For example, 9 has factors 1, 3, 9 — more than two, so it is composite.
  • Every whole number bigger than 1 breaks down into prime factors in exactly one way — its unique "atomic" fingerprint. For example, 12 = 2 × 2 × 3, and no other set of primes multiplies to 12.
  • HCF (Highest Common Factor) is the largest number that divides two numbers exactly — for example, the HCF of 12 and 18 is 6.
  • LCM (Lowest Common Multiple) is the smallest number that both numbers divide into exactly — for example, the LCM of 4 and 6 is 12.
From prime factorsHCF = primes in BOTH
LCM = highest power of EACH
HCF × LCM = the two numbers multiplied
🚌 Bus timetables

Bus A leaves every 12 minutes, Bus B every 15 minutes. They next leave together after the LCM of 12 and 15 = 60 minutes.

🍬 Packing party bags

You have 24 lollies and 36 chocolates to pack into identical bags with nothing left over. The most bags you can make is the HCF of 24 and 36 = 12 (2 lollies + 3 chocolates each).

🧱 Tiling a floor

A floor is 24 tiles by 18 tiles. The largest square block pattern that fits evenly uses the HCF of 24 and 18 = 6.

Why: the HCF is the biggest number that fits into both; the LCM is the first multiple both share. Breaking numbers into prime building blocks makes both easy to find.

MediumMixed method
Find the HCF and LCM of 18 and 24.
  1. Prime factorise each: 18 = 2 × 3 × 3 and 24 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3.
  2. HCF = the primes in BOTH = 2 × 3 = 6.
  3. LCM = the highest power of every prime = 2³ × 3² = 8 × 9 = 72.
  4. Check: HCF × LCM = 6 × 72 = 432 = 18 × 24. ✓
AnswerHCF = 6, LCM = 72

🕵️ Mistake detective

Diagnose the misconception, then reveal the cause and intervention.

They said 1 is a prime number.
Cause

A prime needs exactly two different factors. 1 has only one factor — itself.

Diagnostic question

What rule or representation would show why this answer cannot be right?

Intervention

1 is neither prime nor composite. The smallest prime is 2.
