📖 Read the chapter

A letter stands for an unknown or changing number. We collect like terms, expand brackets and substitute values — the grammar of algebra.

A variable is a letter standing in for a number that is unknown or can change.

  • A term is one piece of an expression, like 3x; the number in front (3) is its coefficient.
  • Only like terms — terms with exactly the same letter part and the same power — can be combined: 3x + 5x = 8x, but 3x + 2y (different letters) and x + x² (different powers, since x² means x×x) cannot be combined.
  • Expanding a bracket means multiplying every term inside it by what's outside: a(b + c) = ab + ac. If the outside number is negative, every term inside flips sign: −2(x − 3) = −2x + 6, not −2x − 6.
  • Factorising is expanding in reverse: find the highest common factor of the terms and write it outside a bracket. 6x + 9 share a common factor of 3, so 6x + 9 = 3(2x + 3). Check by expanding back: 3×2x + 3×3 = 6x + 9. ✓
  • Two expressions are equivalent if they give the same value for every number substituted in — a fact you can spot-check by trying a number in both.
The engine: distributive propertya(b + c) = ab + ac  (expanding — and, reversed, factoring)
−2(x − 3) = −2x + 6  (a negative outside flips every sign inside)
📱 Phone data plan

A plan costs $20 base plus $5 per GB used. The cost for g GB is the expression 20 + 5g. Using 6 GB: 20 + 5×6 = $50.

🚕 Taxi fare

A taxi charges a $4 flagfall plus $2 per kilometre: 4 + 2k. A 9 km trip costs 4 + 2×9 = $22.

🍊 Packing lunchboxes

Packing x apples, and 4 more bananas than apples, means total fruit is x + (x + 4) = 2x + 4 — collecting the two like terms (x and x) into 2x.

Why only add like terms? 3a + 5a is 8 lots of a. But 3a + 2b mixes two different things — like apples and bananas.
⚠ Watch out3(x + 4) = 3x + 12, not 3x + 4 — multiply every term. And 2 + 3x cannot be “simplified” to 5x.
⚡ Mental maths strategySubstitute with invisible brackets: x = −2 in 3x² means 3 × (−2)² = 12 — not −12.

🕵️ Mistake detective

Diagnose the misconception, then reveal the cause and intervention.

3a + 2b simplified to 5ab.
Cause

3a and 2b are not like terms — different letters can't be combined.

Diagnostic question

What rule or representation would show why this answer cannot be right?

Intervention

You can only add like terms, so 3a + 2b is already fully simplified.
